FAQs

What is TWIPLA?

TWIPLA is a platform that connects postgraduate students with investors and banks to facilitate innovative projects through pitching events.

How can I participate in pitching events?

To participate, submit your business idea through the relevant form for entrepreneurs, and prepare for the upcoming pitch events.

What types of projects are accepted?

TWIPLA accepts innovative projects from various fields, including technology, healthcare, sustainability, and more.
